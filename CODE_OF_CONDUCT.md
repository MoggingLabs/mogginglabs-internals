# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want an open, welcoming, and harassment-free environment for everyone who contributes to
MoggingLabs Internals, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at the contact in
[SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md). Maintainers will review and respond to reports and may remove,
edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1.
